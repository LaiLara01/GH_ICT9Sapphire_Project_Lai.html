About Us

We are an organization that teaches people how to not start fires. We do this by providing educational materials and instruction on basic safety precautions. Our goal is to help people avoid injuries and damage caused by fire. Our services are free and available to all members of the community. We believe that with proper education, anyone can learn how to be fire safe.

Contact us today to learn how we can help you!


History

It started in the early 1900s when there was an increase in fires in residential and commercial buildings. People started to realize that they needed to learn how to avoid fires in their homes and places of business. So, we started an organization dedicated to teaching people how to prevent fires. This organization today helps to educate thousands of people every year.
